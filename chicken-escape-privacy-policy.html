Privacy Policy for Chicken Escape

Effective date: May 2026

Chicken Escape does not require users to create an account and does not ask for name, email, phone number, location, camera access, microphone access, or contacts.

Information We Collect

Chicken Escape does not directly collect personal information from users. The app may use Firebase services provided by Google to help improve app stability, understand general app usage, and diagnose crashes or technical issues.

Firebase and Third-Party Services

Chicken Escape may use Firebase services such as Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, or Firebase Performance Monitoring. These services may collect certain app, device, diagnostic, usage, and crash information, such as device type, operating system version, app version, session events, crash logs, and performance data.

This information is used to understand how the app is used, improve game performance, fix bugs, and provide a better user experience. We do not use this information to personally identify users.

Firebase is provided by Google. You can learn more about Firebase privacy and security from Google at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

You can also learn more about how Google uses information from apps and services that use Google technologies at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Advertising

Chicken Escape currently does not show ads. If ads are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated to explain what advertising services are used and what data may be collected.

Children’s Privacy

Chicken Escape is designed to be family-friendly. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

Support Contact

If you contact us for support, we may use your email address only to respond to your request.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
