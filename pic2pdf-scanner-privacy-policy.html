Privacy Policy for Pic2PDF Scanner

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pic2PDF Scanner handles information when you use the mobile application. Pic2PDF Scanner is developed by Javier Guerrero.

1. App Information

App name: Pic2PDF Scanner
Package name: com.javierguerrero.pic2pdfscanner
Developer: Javier Guerrero
Contact email: chinoto.bass@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Pic2PDF Scanner is designed to convert images selected by the user into PDF files. The app does not require account registration and does not ask users to provide personal information such as name, address, phone number, or login credentials.

The app may access images, photos, documents, and folders only when you choose to select them, capture them, or save generated PDF files. This access is used only to provide the app’s core functionality.

3. Photos, Images, and PDF Files

Photos and images selected by the user are processed on the device to create PDF files. Generated PDFs are saved on the device in the location selected by the user or in the app’s default storage location.

Pic2PDF Scanner does not upload your photos or generated PDF files to a server controlled by the developer. Files remain on your device unless you choose to share them using Android sharing options, such as email, messaging apps, cloud storage apps, or other installed applications.

4. Permissions Used by the App

Depending on the features used and your Android version, the app may request access to:

  • Camera, if you choose to take a photo inside the app.
  • Selected photos or images, if you choose images from your device.
  • Selected folders or file locations, if you choose where to save PDF files.

These permissions are used only to allow you to create, save, open, and share PDF files.

5. How We Use Information

The app uses selected images and files only for the following purposes:

  • Creating PDF files from selected photos or images.
  • Applying selected options such as image enhancement, watermark, page numbers, or PDF quality.
  • Saving generated PDF files to your device.
  • Opening, renaming, deleting, or sharing PDFs created by the app.

6. Data Sharing

Pic2PDF Scanner does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

The app does not share your photos or PDF files with the developer. If you choose to share a PDF using Android’s share feature, the file may be sent to the app or service you select. That third-party app or service is governed by its own privacy policy.

7. Third-Party Services

The current version of Pic2PDF Scanner does not require account login, analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud upload services.

If future versions add third-party services such as advertising, analytics, cloud backup, or crash reporting, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe those services and the data they may process.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Generated PDF files are stored on your device until you delete them. You can delete PDFs from inside the app when this feature is available, or by using your device’s file manager.

If you uninstall the app, files stored in the app-specific storage area may be removed by Android. Files saved to a user-selected folder may remain on your device until you delete them manually.

9. Security

Pic2PDF Scanner processes files locally on your device. The app uses Android system features such as file picker, folder picker, and sharing intents to help protect access to your files.

No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but the app is designed to limit access only to the files and folders selected by the user.

10. Children’s Privacy

Pic2PDF Scanner is a general utility app and is not specifically directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
